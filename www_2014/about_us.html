About Us

BandRich Inc., headquarters in Taipei, Taiwan, dedicates itself to providing top value of wireless mobile broadband over 4G LTE, DC-HSPA+/HSPA and EVDO.

The company was established in March 2006 by an industry-renowned management team and continues to be funded by powerful investors. It has achieved sound corporate governance and won several world-class design awards.

As a technology-driven company with brilliant wireless engineers and quality manufacturing, we deliver our customers with state-of-the-art LTE routers and best-in-class outdoor CPEs making fast, reliable, and persistent wireless services easily available.

Vision

The rapid 4G LTE and 3G DC-HSPA+ / HSPA / UMTS capabilities realize faster and permanent data connection without wires. The rich bandwidth with mobility can trigger many new services and applications that bring significant benefits to life, or even evolve into a revolution.

BandRich prospects this change, and is actively developing innovative products in these new applications via broad pipe cellular technology.

BandRich is positioned to be the leader/enabler in the 4G LTE fat pipe.
