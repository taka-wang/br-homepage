R550 VoIP Home Router
LTE WiFi VoIP Router, Home Gateway

Bandluxe R550 series Home Router is an easy-to-install, plug-and-play 4G broadband home/SOHO gateway. With embedded high quality 4G LTE with 3G/2G fallback modem and high sensitivity receiving capability, wireless Internet connectivity to the home or small office is made easily available, saving the costly landline access installation fees. Indoor connectivity is provided by advanced WiFi access and 4 RJ-45 Ethernet LAN ports both supporting data speed up to 100Mbps.

Bandluxe R550 device has a delicately designed glossy surface of gentle concave curve on the bottom edge, standing firm and upright on your table, taking only a small footprint. By placing it in the center or a suitable spot of your home/office, it provides you up to 100Mbps high speed Internet access capability shared by every member of the home/office with strong WiFi signal and Ethernet connectivity. Just plug in the power, enter the password for your home SSID (SSID/password configurable by the user), there you go on the Internet high way without installation efforts—that we have made automatic and inter-operable with your operator.

Bandluxe R550 series, the smart home/SOHO’s wireless Internet sharing solution is quality assured to meet the international standards. It is made simple and automatic for the plug-and-play user, but also made for the expert grade user--providing advanced home router features, as IPv6, Guest Network, WPS, DDNS, configurable LAN/WAN port, dynamic routing, DMZ, UPnP etc. It is your smart choice--take it and enjoy it.

Form Factor

  • Dimension: 143.6 x 73.3 x 143 (LxWxH, mm)
  • Weight (g): 180 g

Connectivity and Data Speed

  • B1 (2100 MHz), B2 (1900 MHz), B3 (1800 MHz), B4 (AWS), B5 (850 MHz), B7 (2600 MHz), Band 8 (900 MHz), B12 (700 MHz), B13 (700 MHz), Band 14 (700 MHz), B17 (700 MHz), B20 (EDD800 MHz), B25 (1900 MHz), Band 26 (800 MHz), B38 (2500 MHz), B40 (2300 MHz), Band 41 (2500 MHz)--Multiple band sku combinations for world-wide usage and welcome for customized band sku discussion.
  • LTE Data Rate: DL up to 100 Mbps, UL up to 50 Mbps
  • LTE Bandwidth: up to 20 MHz
  • EVDO Rev.A: 800MHz/AWS/1900MHz fallback supported
  • UMTS/HSPA+: 850 MHz/900 MHz/AWS/1900 MHz/2100 MHz fallback supported
  • WLAN: 802.11b/g/n, 2x2

Device Interface

  • Power On/Off Switch
  • Power Supply Plug
  • AC adaptor input 100-240V, 50-60Hz AC, Output 12 DC
  • RJ 45 ports: each with LED indicator
  • LED Status Indication
    1. 1 x 4-color Network Status(4G, 3G, 2G service)
    2. 1 x WiFi (multiplexed with signal strength)
    3. 1 x WPS (multiplexed with signal strength)
    4. 1 x Power (multiplexed with signal strength)
    5. 5 x Signal Strength (multiplexed LED)
  • SS/WPS/Reset Button:
    1. short press for signal strength
    2. long press (>3s) for WPS activation
    3. long press (>10s) for reset to factory default setting
