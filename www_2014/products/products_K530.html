K530 Series
M2M/Vehicle Mount Router

Bandluxe K530 Series LTE based high-speed router is purposely built for mission-critical m2m/vehicle use. The transmission speed over 4G LTE is up to 100Mbps downlink and 50Mbps uplink. It is highly ruggedized for your outdoor or indoor application under harsh environments. With the multi-finned housing of aluminum-based cast alloy and the robust design for the interior, it is made to sustain and operate well in the extreme conditions—temperatures ranging from -200 C to +700 C, MIL 810G military standards of shock and vibration, and IP64 grade of dust and water resistance.

Bandluxe K530’s compact yet robust design is ideal for field deployment on various platforms--vehicle, land and maritime transportation, industrial automation, machine-to-machine communication, or enterprise retail transaction that require a full-time reliable wireless connectivity. Its software is tailored to manage your business requirements from center to remote like fleet management, field automation, smart utility, military and police operation, real-time messaging and digital signage, etc.

In addition to maximizing your 24x7 high-speed always-on communication demand, Bndluxe K530 series m2m/vehicle router allows you to easily manage multiple embedded functions for various use cases:

  • IPSec VPN concurrent client sessions for secure access and transmission of sensitive enterprise data
  • GPS plus Glonass faster and double accurate satellite information for better geo-services and location based applications
  • Over the air FW upgrade and device management
  • Configurable WAN/LAN Ethernet interface and various dynamic routing and static routing functions.
  • Router NAT mode and Bridge mode support
  • Firewall and associated port management features
  • Secure Wifi connectivity—WPA/WPA2/TKIP/AES Personal and Enterprise mode
  • Debug tools—AT command, cellular network information, system logs, ping test, trace route, etc.

Form Factor

  • Dimension: 160 x 94 x 44 (L x W x H, mm)
  • Weight: 530 g

Connectivity and Data Speed

  • B1 (2100 MHz), B2 (1900 MHz), B3 (1800 MHz), B4 (AWS), B5 (850 MHz), B7 (2600 MHz), Band 8 (900 MHz), B12 (700 MHz), B13 (700 MHz), Band 14 (700 MHz), B17 (700 MHz), B20 (EDD800 MHz), B25 (1900 MHz), Band 26 (800 MHz), B38 (2500 MHz), B40 (2300 MHz), Band 41 (2500 MHz)--Multiple band sku combinations for world-wide usage and welcome for customized band sku discussion.
  • LTE Data Rate: DL up to 100 Mbps, UL up to 50 Mbps
  • LTE Bandwidth: up to 20 MHz
  • EVDO Rev.A: 800MHz/AWS/1900MHz fallback supported
  • UMTS/HSPA+: 850 MHz/900 MHz/AWS/1900 MHz/2100 MHz fallback supported
  • Customizable preferred radio network selection priority

Device Interface

  • 2 x Cellular antenna ports (SMA) for LTE MIMO
  • 2 x WiFi antenna port (SMA) for 802.11 b/g/n, 2 x 2
  • 1 x GPS antenna port (SMA)--supporting 3.3VDC active antenna (GPS plus GLONASS)
  • 2 x RJ-45 ports, 10/100 LAN
  • 1 SIM slot
  • 1 x USB port (USB storage, power source, USB to serial adaptor)
  • 6 x LEDs
    1. cellular (blue: 4G; green: 3G; purple: 2G; red: SIM error or service failure)
    2. signal strength (tir-color)
    3. LAN1
    4. LAN2
    5. WiFi
    6. GPS
  • Input power 12V or 24V expected, accepting 9~32 V voltage swing

Wireless LAN

  • 802.11b/g/n, 2x2 MIMO
  • Security Types:
    1. WPA/WPA2 AES/TKIP mode encryption
    2. WPA/WPA2 PSK, WPA/WPA2 Enterprise
    3. None/64/128 bits WEP encryption
    4. Open system authentication
  • Device unique default encryption: linked to serial number of the device
  • Channel Selection: Auto or Manual
  • WLAN on/off software switch
  • SSID broadcast disable, dual SSID, Access control (MAC filtering)

Routing Features

  • IPv4 and IPv6
  • Static Routing, Dynamic Routing (RIP, BGP, OSPF)
  • Single Port Forwarding, Port Range Forwarding, Port Range Triggering, Port Filtering, IP Filtering, DMZ, UPnP, Multicast Pass-Through
  • Stateless and SPI Firewall, Internet Filter, Web Filter
  • VPN client mode (PPTP, IPSec, SSL), Multiple VPN pass-through (IPSec, PPTP, L2TP)
  • DNS Agent, DDNS
  • TCP, UDP, ICMP, ARP, DHCP Server/Client, DHCP Reservation, HTTP/HTTPs, NTP, ALGs
  • NAT mode and Bridge mode supported

SW Features

  • Over the air FW upgrade, device management
  • Connection on demand, Auto Connection, Auto APN matching with USIM, APN database update through browser-based GUI, APN profile, PIN management, Preferred radio NW type selection
  • Network name, Signal strength, Roaming indication, Radio technology, Connection status, Connection time, Connection Statistics
  • NMEA info supplied for GPS
  • GPS data broadcast in IP packets in LAN and WiFi, GPS location shown on GUI

  • Storage Temperature: -40°C to 85°C (-40°F to 185°F)
  • Operating Humidity: 10% to 85% non-condensing
  • Storage Humidity: 5% to 90% non-condensing
