BandRich and Far EasTone Telecom Launch BandLuxe C120 HSDPA USB Modem in Taiwan
2007-11/15
Taipei, Taiwan, Feb 5th ??BandRich Inc. and Far EasTone Telecom announced the launch of BandLuxe® C120 HSDPA USB dongle modem at Far EasTone's winter campaign. The BandLuxe® C120 is now available through Far EasTone's retail stores and business sales channels, offering simple and high speed mobile broadband through the operator's high quality network.

The BandLuxe® C120 is designed for compact USB stick form-factor. Measuring only 88x28x17.4mm, it can be carried comfortably in the pocket, without the hassle of USB extension cable. For use with desktop or laptop at home, in the office, or on the road, the BandLuxeR C120 provides Far EasTone customers with plug-and-play mobile broadband access for internet surfing, emailing and instant message chatting.

The user-friendly design of the BandLuxe® C120 facilitates a CD-free installation process. The client applications, drivers and manual will be installed automatically when the device connected to the computer for the first time. The operator connection settings would be automatically configured via recognizing the USIM inserted.

" BandRich is pleased to collaborate again with Far EasTone in offering Taiwan customers high quality and innovative mobile broadband experience," said Dr. Wen-Yi Kuo, BandRich's co-founder and CEO. " We appreciate the prior customer applause to our C100 HSDPA ExpressCard data card and we keep the high quality and radio performance legend into this new USB-stick C120 data modem. With this new form factor, the users are offered with more attractive size and can be free from the annoying USB cable."

The BandLuxe® C120 provides worldwide roaming with supports for quad band EDGE/GPRS connectivity (850/900/1800/1900) and 2100MHz UMTS/HSDPA connectivity. With the state-of-the-art HSDPA technology, BandLuxeR C120 offers data speed up to 7.2Mbps.

For more information about the BandLuxe® C120 and other BandLuxeR family products, please visit http://www.bandrich.com/products.aspx?lang=en-US
